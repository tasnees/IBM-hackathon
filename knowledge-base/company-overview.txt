# TechNova Solutions - Company Overview

## About TechNova Solutions

**TechNova Solutions** is a global enterprise technology company founded in 2008, headquartered in Austin, Texas. We specialize in providing cutting-edge software solutions, cloud infrastructure, and data analytics platforms to Fortune 500 companies worldwide.

### Company Facts
- **Founded:** 2008
- **Headquarters:** Austin, TX
- **Employees:** 12,500+
- **Global Offices:** 15 countries
- **Annual Revenue:** $4.2 Billion
- **Stock Symbol:** TNVA (NASDAQ)

| Department Code | Department Name | Description |
