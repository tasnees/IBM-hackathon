## Method 2: Find Product ID in the Application

Most TechNova applications display the Product ID in one of these locations:

### Desktop Applications
1. Open the TechNova application
2. Click **Help** → **About** in the menu bar
3. Look for "Product ID" or "Application ID" in the dialog

### Web Applications
1. Log into the TechNova web application
2. Click on your **profile icon** in the top right corner
3. Select **About** or **System Information**
4. The Product ID is displayed under "Application Details"

### Mobile Applications
1. Open the TechNova mobile app
2. Tap the **hamburger menu** (☰) or **Settings gear** (⚙️)
3. Scroll to **About** or **App Info**
4. The Product ID is listed under version information

---

## Method 3: Check Your License or Subscription

Your Product ID can be found in your TechNova license documentation:

1. Log into the **TechNova Customer Portal**: https://portal.technovasolutions.com
2. Navigate to **My Subscriptions** or **My Licenses**
3. Each product will show its Product ID in the format: `DEPT-###`
4. Example: `CLOUD-001`, `DATA-002`, `SEC-005`

   - Say "Product ID lookup" when prompted
   - A support agent will help identify your product

2. **Chat Support:** https://support.technovasolutions.com/chat
   - Available 24/7

3. **Email Support:** producthelp@technovasolutions.com
   - Include a screenshot or description of the application
   - Response within 2 business hours

## Product ID Format Explained

All TechNova Product IDs follow this format:

```
[DEPARTMENT]-[NUMBER]
```

| Part | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| DEPARTMENT | 3-7 letter department code | CLOUD, DATA, SEC, COLLAB, FIN, DEV, ITSM, ERP |
| NUMBER | 3-digit product number (001-999) | 001, 002, 003... |

**Examples:**
- `CLOUD-001` = NovaSphere VM (Cloud Infrastructure)
- `DATA-002` = NovaBI Studio (Data & Analytics)
- `SEC-003` = NovaIdentity (Cybersecurity)

---

## Next Steps

Once you have your Product ID, you're ready to create a ServiceNow incident:

1. Go to **TechNova ServiceNow Portal**: https://technova.service-now.com
2. Click **Create Incident**
3. Enter your **Product ID** in the "Configuration Item" or "Product" field
4. Describe your issue
5. Submit the incident

Your incident will be automatically routed to the correct support team based on the Product ID you provided.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if my product isn't listed?**
A: Contact our support team at 1-800-TECHNOVA. You may be using a custom or legacy product that requires manual routing.

**Q: Can I use multiple Product IDs in one incident?**
A: No. If your issue affects multiple products, create separate incidents for each product. This ensures each team can properly address their component.

**Q: What if I enter the wrong Product ID?**
A: Don't worry! Our support team will reassign the incident to the correct team if needed. However, using the correct Product ID from the start ensures faster resolution.

**Q: I'm not sure if it's a product issue or user error.**
A: Create the incident anyway! Our support team will help determine the root cause and provide guidance regardless.
